Why Pigeon Post?

Slow Down the Conversation

In a world of read receipts and instant replies, there's something magical about waiting. Pigeon Post embraces intentional delay—giving both sender and receiver time to savor the anticipation.

Digital, But Personal

No stamps, no postal delays, no lost mail—but still that feeling of crafting something just for someone. Pick your design, write your message, choose when it arrives.

Built with AI, Designed by Hand

A passion project built in spare time, using modern AI tools to accelerate development while staying true to craft in every design decision.
