The Challenge

Gween Toys is a pro-environment toy brand based in Bengaluru, India. They started making eco-friendly and child-safe wooden toys in 2011, but had to shut operations due to demand/supply mismatch. In 2020, when India's Prime Minister announced that import taxes on toys would increase exponentially, Gween saw an opportunity to revive and expand their inventory from 5 to 30 toys in a single quarter.

Channapatna: 300 Years of Craft

Not many toys can boast of having a 300-year old history that began because of a king. Channapatna, located in rural Karnataka about 100km from the main city, is home to workshops inside normal homes where skilled artisans turn wood into beautiful toys using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

Artisans Hand Crafting Toys in Channapatna
Visit to Channapatna
To better understand the situation and constraints, we took a trip to Channapatna before diving into design. The visit uncovered crucial insights about the artisans' capabilities, material limitations, and the economic pressures facing second-generation craftspeople.

Design Philosophy

Every Gween toy is designed to entertain and educate children while communicating a message about saving the environment. We focused on creating toys that encourage open-ended play, build developmental skills, and connect children with nature through sustainable materials.
