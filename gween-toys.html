Gween Toys

High-quality, organic toys crafted from natural materials by traditional artisans. I led the product expansion for this pro-environment toy brand, growing their catalog from 5 to 30 toys while preserving the 300-year-old Channapatna craft tradition from rural Karnataka, India.

Key Decisions

Artisan-First Documentation

Traditional technical drawings didn't work for artisans in Channapatna. I created visual design specs with color-coded annotations and 3D renders that could be understood without reading, bridging the gap between digital design and traditional craft.

Modular Play Systems

Rather than standalone toys, I designed modular systems like Wobots (mix & match robots) and Dwino (word-forming dinosaur) that encourage creative recombination, extending play value while keeping production simple for artisans.

Preserving Craft Identity

Channapatna toys have a distinct lacquer finish and turned-wood aesthetic. Every new design honored these constraints, using traditional techniques like lac coating while introducing contemporary play patterns.

Developmental Play Focus

Each toy was designed around specific developmental skills: thumb-finger grasp, cause-and-effect understanding, balance, and fine motor control. This gave parents clear reasons to choose handcrafted over plastic.
