Auto-Translate

Auto-Translate for Adobe Express enables users to localize their designs instantly—translating text across pages while preserving layout, styling, and design intent. Announced at Adobe MAX 2023, the feature was key to expanding Express's global reach: with a template library built primarily in English, Auto-Translate became the entry point for users in non-English speaking markets to access and adapt thousands of professionally designed templates in their own language.

Key Decisions

Unlocking the Template Library for the World

Express had thousands of beautifully designed templates—but they were predominantly in English, limiting their value for users in Japan, Brazil, Germany, and beyond. Auto-Translate transformed every English template into a globally accessible starting point, effectively multiplying the library's reach without requiring localized versions of each asset.

Preserving Design Intent

Translation isn't just swapping words. German runs longer than English; Japanese changes text direction. The system intelligently reflows text while respecting the creator's original layout decisions—scaling, wrapping, and adjusting without breaking the design.

Closing the Loop with User Feedback

To continuously improve translation quality, we designed a lightweight feedback mechanism that captures user sentiment without disrupting their workflow. Users can rate translations and flag issues, feeding directly into our quality metrics and model improvements.

Context-Aware Translation (Patent Filed)

Traditional translators fail on ambiguous words—translating "bow" as the verb when the image shows a bow-and-arrow. Our LLM-powered system uses visual context from the document to disambiguate meaning, delivering translations that are not just linguistically accurate but contextually appropriate. This innovation is now patent-pending.

The Experience

Translate with precision

Pick your languages, set the tone, and choose exactly what to translate. Select entire pages or drill into specific text boxes—keep your brand name in English while localizing everything else. When you're ready, hit Translate.

No waiting around

Translation takes time, especially for larger files. Instead of blocking you with a loading screen, we hand it off to AI in the background. Click away to keep working on other projects—your translated file will be ready when you are.

See everything at a glance

When translation is complete, review all your pages in one view. Scan for anything that needs tweaking before you export or share.

Layout intact, language transformed

Text length changes, characters shift—but the design holds. No manual cleanup required. German, Japanese, Spanish—the layout adapts intelligently to preserve your original design intent.
